Who We Are

Leading the Global Sustainability Revolution

INNOVORA EcoHub Group stands at the intersection of innovation and environmental stewardship. We are a community of young changemakers, environmental enthusiasts, and sustainability champions working together to address the pressing challenges of our time.

"Waste is not the end of a product's life—it is the beginning of new possibilities."

Our mission is to create sustainable solutions that benefit both people and the planet. Through education, innovation, and community engagement, we are building a future where sustainability is not just a goal—it's a way of life. With operations spanning multiple continents, INNOVORA is redefining what it means to be a sustainable corporation.

From Waste to Value

Our flagship project focuses on transforming cocoa husks—agricultural waste typically discarded during cocoa processing—into sustainable, high-value products. This initiative has multiple benefits:

  • Environmental Impact: Reduces agricultural waste and deforestation pressure
  • Economic Opportunity: Creates income streams for farmers and local communities
  • Innovation: Develops new sustainable materials and products
  • Youth Engagement: Provides training and employment for young people
